Pancakes

This is a recipe for pancakes that I have been using for years. They are thick, fluffy, and you can add fruit or chocolate chips to the batter if you want to switch it up.

Quick Facts

  • Prep Time: 10 min
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Additional Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 30 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

Part 1

In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

Part 2

Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop
